About Me

I am a researcher in mathLab at the International School for Advanced Studies (SISSA). Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Florence. My research interests encompass numerical analysis, approximation theory, splines, fractals, their interactions, and practical applications. I earned my Ph.D. at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi under the mentorship of Dr. P. Viswanathan.
